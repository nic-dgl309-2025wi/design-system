Branding

Primary Logo

People and birds come together in this updated logo, where the silhouette of a raven, perched on the right, faces a gentle hand holding an oak leaf. This imagery symbolizes the delicate balance between humans, nature, and wildlife. The raven, known for its intelligence and adaptability, represents the resilience of the animals MARS rescues. The cupped hand signifies human care, compassion, and stewardship, while the oak leaf reflects strength, growth, and the natural environment that MARS works to protect. Organic lines in the illustration, combined with a bold yet clean font, create a friendly and inviting impression.

The primary logo will be used for signage, merchandise, digital platforms, and promotional materials. A vertical orientation is preferred to ensure readability and adaptability across these mediums. The logo has a tagline version which is optional and can be included or removed depending on the application.

Horizontal Logo

Horizontal logos are used for navbars and where vertical space is limited, such as on narrow banners, social media profiles, mobile interfaces, or in email signatures, the secondary horizontal logo offers a more practical and visually balanced option. Its streamlined orientation ensures that the logo remains clear and recognizable, even when displayed at smaller sizes or in constrained layouts.

The logo has a tagline version which is optional and can be included or removed depending on the application.

Logo Icons

In situations where space is limited and small details may be lost, the icon logo works best. Uses for this logo include event signage and name tags, social media profile pictures, stickers and promotional items.

You can also find this logo mark on wayfinding such as the front door and directional signage, all throughout social media, and available for sale on unique merchandise. The text-free logo icon does not have a tagline version.
